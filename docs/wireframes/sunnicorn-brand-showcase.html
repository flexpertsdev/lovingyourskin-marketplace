Our Circular Beauty Revolution

Every SUNNICORN product tells a story of transformation - from rescued ugly food ingredients to recycled packaging, we're redefining what sustainable vegan beauty means for sensitive skin.

Ugly Food Heroes

We rescue perfectly good produce rejected for cosmetic imperfections, extracting potent active vegetable ingredients that would otherwise go to waste from clean agricultural environments.

True Upcycling

From performance to package, we practice genuine upcycling by processing excess plastics and glass to create eco-friendly cosmetic containers designed for disposal consideration.

Sensitive Skin Focus

Continuous research and development for sensitive skin through vegan formulations, ensuring our sustainable ingredients are gentle yet effective for all skin types.

From Rejected to Respected

These "imperfect" ingredients are nutritional powerhouses, grown in clean environments but rejected simply for their irregular shapes. We see their true beauty and potential.

Our Philosophy

We use ugly food which failed to reach the market due to their irregular, ugly shapes despite being grown in clean environments. These fresh vegetables contain the same powerful active ingredients as their "perfect" counterparts, making them ideal for sensitive skin formulations while reducing agricultural waste.
