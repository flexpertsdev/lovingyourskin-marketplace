LYS Platform Terms of Service

Clear, plain English terms governing all platform relationships

1. Platform Role & Services

LYS Platform Limited ("LYS", "we", "us") operates as a B2B marketplace facilitating transactions between Korean beauty brands ("Brands") and European Union retailers ("Retailers").

  • Payment Facilitation: We process payments via Stripe Connect and hold funds in escrow until shipment confirmation
  • Communication Hub: We facilitate initial communication between Brands and Retailers
  • Order Management: We provide the platform infrastructure for order submission, tracking, and status updates
  • Compliance Support: We handle international payment compliance and documentation requirements

2. Payment Terms & Processing

All financial transactions are processed in USD through our Stripe Connect integration.

  • Retailer Payments: Must be made via bank transfer before any goods are shipped. Payment terms of up to 30 days may be offered, but shipment will not commence until payment is received and confirmed.
  • Escrow System: All payments are held in LYS's platform account until shipment confirmation is provided by the Brand.
  • Brand Payouts: Upon valid shipment confirmation (including tracking number), funds are automatically transferred to Brand's Stripe account within 24 hours.
  • Platform Fees: LYS charges a fixed fee per item (disclosed at time of order). All Stripe processing fees are absorbed by LYS.
  • Currency Risk: Each party bears their own currency conversion risk (EUR→USD for Retailers, USD→KRW for Brands).

3. Liability & Risk Allocation

Liability is clearly allocated based on order stage:

  • Pre-Shipment: LYS holds funds in escrow and may refund Retailers if Brands fail to ship confirmed orders.
  • Upon Shipment Confirmation: ALL liability for product quality, delivery, customer service, and commercial disputes transfers directly to the Brand.
  • Brand Indemnification: Upon providing shipment confirmation, Brands agree to indemnify LYS against any claims, damages, or losses arising from product defects, delivery failures, or commercial disputes.
  • LYS Limitation: After shipment confirmation, LYS's role is limited to facilitating communication. We are not liable for product quality, delivery issues, or commercial disputes.

4. Import Compliance & Customs

  • Retailer Responsibility: Retailers are solely responsible for all import duties, VAT payments, customs compliance, and regulatory requirements in their jurisdiction.
  • Brand Documentation: Brands must provide accurate commercial invoices, certificates of origin, and other required export documentation.
  • Customs Delays: Neither LYS nor Brands are liable for customs delays, additional duties, or regulatory compliance issues in the destination country.

5. Dispute Resolution

  • Pre-Shipment Disputes: LYS will mediate and may refund payments if Brands fail to meet confirmed obligations.
  • Post-Shipment Disputes: All quality, delivery, or commercial disputes become direct Brand-Retailer matters. LYS may facilitate initial communication but is not a party to the dispute.
  • Refund Authority: After shipment confirmation, only Brands can authorize refunds, which must be processed directly to Retailers through their own payment methods.
  • Governing Law: These terms are governed by English law. Any disputes involving LYS must be resolved in English courts.

6. Data & Communication

  • Platform Communication: All initial order-related communication flows through LYS platform systems.
  • Direct Contact: After shipment confirmation, Brands and Retailers may communicate directly for ongoing commercial matters.
  • Data Privacy: LYS processes personal and business data in accordance with UK GDPR requirements.
  • Order Records: LYS maintains transaction records for legal and compliance purposes.

7. Termination & Exit

  • Platform Exit: Brands or Retailers may cease using the platform at any time, subject to completing existing orders.
  • Ongoing Obligations: Liability and service obligations for shipped orders continue indefinitely regardless of platform participation.
  • Data Retention: LYS may retain transaction and communication records for legal compliance purposes.
⚠️ Key Legal Points:
  • LYS is a payment facilitator, not a merchant or party to Brand-Retailer sales
  • Shipment confirmation triggers immediate liability transfer to Brands
  • Post-shipment disputes are direct Brand-Retailer commercial matters
  • Each party bears their own currency and regulatory compliance risks
