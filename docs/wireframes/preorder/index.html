Pre-Order FAQs

How does pre-ordering work? +
Pre-ordering allows you to secure products before their official launch. Simply add items to your cart and complete checkout. You'll be charged immediately, and products will ship on the launch date specified for each brand.
When will I receive my pre-ordered items? +
Each brand has its own launch date displayed on the product cards. Your pre-ordered items will ship on or shortly after the launch date. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships. Typical delivery is 3-5 business days after shipping.
Why do I pay now for products that ship later? +
Payment at the time of pre-order secures your items and guarantees the 20% discount. This helps us ensure we have adequate stock for all pre-orders and prevents disappointment when products launch.
Can I cancel or modify my pre-order? +
Pre-orders can be cancelled up to 7 days before the product launch date for a full refund. After this period, orders cannot be cancelled as they enter the fulfillment process. Modifications to shipping addresses can be made up to 48 hours before launch.
Are pre-order items limited in quantity? +
Yes, pre-order quantities are limited based on our exclusive agreements with brands. Once the allocated pre-order stock is sold out, items won't be available until the general launch at full retail price.
Can I combine pre-orders from different brands? +
Yes! You can add pre-order items from multiple brands to your cart. However, each brand's products will ship separately on their respective launch dates. You'll only pay shipping once per brand order.
