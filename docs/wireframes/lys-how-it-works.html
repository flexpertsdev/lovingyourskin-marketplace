Your Order Journey

Day 1: Place Your Order

Select products, meet minimum order quantities, and submit your order through our platform.

Day 1-2: Order Confirmation

We verify your order with the brand and confirm availability. You'll receive updates via in-app messaging.

Day 3: Documentation & Invoice

Receive your final invoice including all costs. We handle all customs documentation for you.

Day 4-5: Payment & Processing

Complete payment through secure channels. Your order is prepared for shipment.

Day 6-15: Shipping & Delivery

Track your shipment in real-time. Receive your products with all necessary documentation.
